Hey! I'm Polly.

I am an UX/UI and visual designer. I like to think of myself as a multifaceted problem-solver. I have had a non-traditional career path working at newspapers, advertising agencies, in-house outdoor companies as well as opening my own business. All of these experiences have led me to where I am now, continuing my education in UX/UI through the University of Utah and working full-time at an amazing specialized design firm in Park City, Utah.

Life has a way of sending you down many different paths–some hard, some easy–but ultimately shaping us to who we are today. I embrace the journey, and learn from all my experiences to become better as a person as well as a professional designer.

I love to balance my time at the computer with being outdoors. I am a sports junkie, if not at my desk you can find me skiing, fishing, running, hiking, biking or playing with all my animals.
